REVIEWER: Nick Clarke

NAME: Dickey's Barbecue Pit

ADDRESS: 799 Foothill Blvd

CITY: , San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 4

VENUE: 3

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW:

Dickey’s Barbecue Pit is a southern style restaurant. You order and receive your food at the counter in an assembly line style. They have a range of specials for each day of the week in addition to a usual range of meat plates and sandwiches. They serve three types of barbecue sauce: original, spicy, and sweet. The store includes a soft serve machine that is freely available to every customer regardless of what they purchased. The meat was cooked slowly, resulting in a great taste. The sides include a variety of options including potato casserole, green beans, french fries, baked beans, and macaroni & cheese. Each side I tried was satisfactory and filling. The menu lacked variety but was very good it what it did provide.

The service was friendly and helpful. I went in the middle of the day when the restaurant was not crowded. The servers were willing to chat with me and answer questions about the different foods they offered. There are no waiters and no busboys. The servers behind the counter seemed very knowledgeable about their job and were willing to recommend choices based on their personal favorites as well as the most popular foods they have served.

The venue was small but adequate. The air conditioning formed an oasis from the heat outside. Country music was playing in the background to build the atmosphere. The tables and floor did not seem especially clean but they had been wiped down. Because it was not crowded, we had our own table, but as we were leaving, more people were entering than available tables. This restaurant is especially popular in takeout and catering, which explains why they only provide a limited eating area.

Overall I give this restaurant a high rating of 4 out of 5. The food is decently priced with a meal around $10. Although not entirely authentic, it does a good job of replicating southern cuisine. It is a casual restaurant with a laidback atmosphere offering reasonably priced food. I would definitely return.
