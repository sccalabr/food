REVIEWER: Taylor Nesheim
NAME: Jaffa Cafe
ADDRESS: 1308 Monterey Street
CITY: San Luis Obispo
FOOD: 5
SERVICE: 5
VENUE: 5
RATING: 5
WRITTEN REVIEW:
The food at Jaffa Cafe is in a word amazing. It has the most delicious lamb gyros that I have ever tasted.
The service at Jaffa Cafe is a bit unorthodox but still good. You order first, get your food, then pay after you eat. Because of this level of customer trust I feel obligated to give them a five star rating.
They recently moved from an older and smaller building to their new place. It made a huge difference, in particular there are much more places to park now then there were before.
I would rate this restaurant with top marks. They have friendly staff, good ambience, and again their food is simply to die for.
